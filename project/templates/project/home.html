How to Rent an Item

  1. Choose Your Item: Browse or search for an item that suits your needs. Click on the "Rent" button to start the rental process.
  2. Pick Sizes and Quantities: If the item has sizes (e.g., XS, S, M, L, XL), select the quantities you need for each size. If it's a prop, just choose the total quantity.
  3. Payment: Rentals are honor-based and managed via Venmo. Use the organization's Venmo username displayed on the checkout page. Please confirm with the recipient to make sure your payment has gone through before confirming your order!
  4. Order Completion: Once the rental period is over:
    • As the Renter: Orders will be marked as overdue until they are returned to the seller.
    • As the Seller: Ensure the item is returned in good condition and mark the order as complete to finalize the transaction.
